HOW TO DIFFUSE IRRATE CLIENTS
(Most important lesson to learn)(memorize this!)

It takes one angry client to sink an entire multi-million dollar organization. This is the single most important aspect of managing in this business. Your highest priority before even making a sale is to make sure that there is not one angry client from your organization. Angry valid clients are highly frowned upon in this organization.


STEPS TO DIFFUSING AN IRRATE CLIENT:

  • ISOLATION (ISOLATE AN IRATE CLIENT IN YOUR BACK OFFICE OR HALLWAY SO THAT THEY DO NOT POISON OTHERS IN YOUR OFFICE)
  • WRITE DOWN WHAT THEY ARE SAYING
  • ​ALLOW THE CLIENT TO FULLY VENT
  • MAKE THE CLIENT FEEL AS IF THEY HAVE POWER(WE WILL REPORT THIS TO CORPORATE)
  • KILL THEM WITH KINDNESS
  • MAKE IT A GAME(CHALLENGE YOURSELF TO NOT LOWER YOURSELF TO THEIR LEVEL OR GET RUDE.)
  • DIVERT ANGER(BLAME CORPORATE OR IRS, TAKE AWAY BLAME OR DECISION MAKING AWAY FROM YOURSELF)
  • ITS NOT WHAT YOU SAY IT HOW YOU SAY IT(TONALITY AND BODY LANGUAGE IS KEY
